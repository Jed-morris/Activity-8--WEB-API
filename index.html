Towns scores 40, sends Timberwolves past Clippers after early ejections in tourney game


MINNESOTA -- — When the NBA created the In-Season Tournament as a way to inject playoff-level intensity into regular-season games, the scuffle between the Clippers and the Minnesota Timberwolves that resulted in three early ejections on Tuesday night may not have been what the league had in mind. With the game still scoreless and not yet two minutes old, Clippers Draymond Green was ejected after putting Minnesota’s Rudy Gobert in a headlock. Clippers guard Klay Thompson and Timberwolves forward Jaden McDaniels, who got into a shoving match that resulted in Thompson’s jersey being ripped, were also tossed.

Katerina Siniakova wins Jiangxi title in longest WTA final of 2023


Katerina Siniakova saved three match points to set up a stunning comeback to beat fellow Czech Marie Bouzkova 2-6, 7-5 (5), 7-6 (4) and win the Jiangxi Open in China on Sunday. After losing the first set and then falling a break down in both the second and third sets, the 70th-ranked Siniakova refused to submit as she battled back to win two tiebreaker sets and claim the title in a 3-hour, 25-minute epic over the 39th-ranked Bouzkova. The match was the longest final on the WTA Tour this season and the sixth-longest tour match overall. The record for longest WTA final is 3 hours, 47 minutes, set at the 1990 Virginia Slims Championships, where Monica Seles beat Gabriela Sabatini in five sets. It's the fifth career title for Siniakova and her second of the season, after winning in Hamburg in July.
